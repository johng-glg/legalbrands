Debt collectors have to follow rules. The federal Fair Debt Collection Practices Act
(FDCPA) limits when and how collectors can contact you — and once you're represented by
an attorney, collectors generally must route contact through your counsel.

## Three things to know

1. You can ask collectors to stop contacting you directly.
2. Collectors who break the law can be held accountable.
3. You don't have to face it alone — a free consultation is a no-pressure first step.
