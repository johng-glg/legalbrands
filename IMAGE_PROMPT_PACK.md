# GLG Brand Portfolio — Image Direction & Generative Prompt Pack

Purpose: drive on-brand, **differentiated** imagery across all five sites so they read as separate firms,
not one stock shoot. Each brand has a defined persona, casting/lighting spec graded to its real palette,
and copy-paste prompts for Midjourney + Firefly.

### How to use the two tools
- **Midjourney** — exploration, mood, cinematic hero shots. Use `--style raw` for photographic realism and a
  low stylize value (`--s 50–150`) so it stays believable, not glossy. `--ar 16:9` hero, `--ar 4:5` social.
- **Adobe Firefly** — final, commercially-safe production assets (trained on licensed/owned content, so no
  third-party IP surprises in client-facing marketing). Set Content Type = **Photo**; Firefly reads prompts
  more literally, so the prompts below are written plainer for it.
- **Consistency rule:** append the brand's **Image DNA** line to every prompt in that brand. That single
  string (lighting + grade + mood) is what keeps a brand's library cohesive across dozens of generations.

### Casting principle across all brands
Cast **protagonists, never victims.** No crying, no head-in-hands despair, no shame poses. We sell the
*after* state (relief, control, resolve), not the pain. Cast for real diversity of age, race, and household.

## 1 · Off the Hook Law — the relief brand
**offthehooklaw.com · top-funnel · peace family**

**Persona — "The Exhale."** 38–58, working-to-middle class, $10k–$100k unsecured debt. A parent with kids
still at home, or caring for an aging parent; a teacher, nurse, contractor, retail manager. Anxious,
embarrassed, losing sleep over collector calls — **often not yet sued.** The image must capture the moment
the weight lifts: shoulders dropping, a real breath, the phone finally quiet.

- **Setting:** warm domestic — kitchen table at morning, sofa with coffee, back porch.
- **Wardrobe:** soft, casual, lived-in. Cream, sage, oatmeal tones.
- **Lighting / grade:** warm golden morning light, soft and diffuse; graded toward **Warm Cream #FBF6EE**
  with a quiet **Liberation Green #1E9E6A** environmental note. Never clinical.
- **Shot:** medium, candid, slight genuine smile, a phone set face-down on the table.

**Image DNA (append to every prompt):**
`warm golden morning light, soft diffuse, cream and sage palette, candid relief, authentic, unposed`

**Midjourney — hero:**
`a working mother in her late 40s sitting at a sunlit kitchen table, eyes closed, taking a calm relieved breath, a phone face-down beside her coffee, warm golden morning light, soft diffuse, cream and sage palette, candid relief, authentic, unposed, photographic --style raw --ar 16:9 --s 100`

**Midjourney — social portrait:**
`a relaxed father in his 50s on a back porch at morning, gentle genuine smile, shoulders dropped, warm natural light, cream and sage palette, candid, documentary, photographic --style raw --ar 4:5 --s 100`

**Firefly — hero (Photo):**
`Candid photo of a middle-aged woman at a sunlit kitchen table exhaling with relief, phone face-down nearby, warm morning light, soft cream and sage tones, authentic and unposed, shallow depth of field.`

**Firefly — abstract texture (section background):**
`Soft rounded loop and arc shapes, single-color tints of warm green on cream, minimal, calm, subtle paper texture, no text.`

**Negative / avoid:** `courtroom, suit and tie, gavel, money, cash, distress, crying, stiff stock smile, blue corporate tones, harsh light`

## Production notes
- Generate **abstract texture** shots per brand first — they double as section-divider backgrounds and OG
  cards and are low-risk to produce.
- For hero people-shots, run Midjourney to lock the look, then reproduce the chosen frame in Firefly for the
  final commercially-safe asset you actually ship.
- Keep selects in `/src/brands/<slug>/` per the build spec (`logo.svg`, `og.png`, `hero.jpg`, `secondary.jpg`,
  `texture.svg`). The config's `photography`, `pattern`, and `logoDirection` strings already match these prompts.
- If you want even tighter consistency, lock a single seed per brand in Midjourney and reuse it.
